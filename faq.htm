Why use CPA2GO? 

When your question demands more than just a google answer. When tax law reads like Caesar wrote it. 

What are some examples of questions that CPA2GO answers? 

What is the difference between an LLC and an S Corporation? Now that I am self-employed what can I write off and what documentation do I need to save? Should I buy or lease a vehicle for my business? What new tax law changes will affect me or my business?
      

Does CPA2GO take the place of my existing advisor’s, CPA’s or tax preparer’s? 

No. We are the stop gap, second opinion or quick answer to your important questions. We will point you to a local professional if you need help with your long-term solutions. 

How many questions can I ask CPA2GO? 

There are no limits to how many questions you can ask our licensed CPAs. Each question will be processed individually and can be accessed in your account dashboard. 

How quick will I receive an answer once I have “Asked a Question”? 

Our licensed CPAs process tax questions as they are received. The typical response time is 1-2 hours. 

How is payment processed once I sign up?

Payment information is required to be submitted before asking a question. Payment will be processed once the question is asked. A licensed CPA will then generate a response within a timely manner.
