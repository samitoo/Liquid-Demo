The CPA2GO business model is one of licensing/franchising to local CPA firms and allowing them to serve the market in which they live and work. With a CPA2GO franchise, a CPA firm can add incremental income to their existing business with very little overhead added as they already have the tools and employees to answer questions for their community. In addition to adding substantial profits to the bottom line, they also get exposure to a new generation of business owners who utilize technology and social media more than ever before.
